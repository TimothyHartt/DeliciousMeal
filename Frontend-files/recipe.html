Funny Face Eggs

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Turn stove on to medium heat.
  3. Heat pan up on stove.
  4. Butter the pan.
  5. Crack two eggs in the pan.
  6. Cook eggs for 3 minuets
  7. Plate the eggs.
  8. Put bacon on a cookie sheet.
  9. Place cookie sheet in the oven.
