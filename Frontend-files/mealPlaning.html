Acai Bowl



Recipe:

  1. Fill a medium sized bowl with 3 cups of acai juice
  2. Add 2 cups of granola
  3. Add 1 cup of almonds
  4. Cut up six stawberries and add them to the bowl
  5. Cut up half a Bananna and add it to the bowl
  6. Add a handful of blackberries
  7. Sprinkle coconut shavings over the top
